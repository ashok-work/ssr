FAQs

About EventoLoop
What is EventoLoop?

EventoLoop is an online platform and marketplace for people to instantly list, discover, and book short-term spaces to create unique event experiences.

What does EventoLoop do?

EventoLoop connects owners of one-of-a-kind spaces with people looking to create event experiences for small gatherings, workshops, photo-shoots, exhibitions, company off-sites, and more. Users can also search for event production services, making Splacer a one-stop shop for all event planning needs. Splacer’s purpose is twofold: providing event hosts with a platform for finding creative spaces that were previously unavailable, and giving space owners an opportunity to put their underutilized spaces to good use, make some extra cash, showcase their space and expand their network.

How does EventoLoop find spaces?

EventoLoop utilizes a network of local scouts and affiliates to access unique spaces and curates its offering to ensure only the best venues are listed on the platform.

How often does EventoLoop list new spaces on the site?

Almost daily. EventoLoop’s database is constantly updated. Check in every few days to see new additions.

How does EventoLoop differ from other platforms?

Unlike other online event platforms, EventoLoop curates its spaces: all spaces are reviewed by the EventoLoop team that then select only genuinely unique locations. EventoLoop was founded by a group of architects and creative minds who work to put together a truly outstanding list of unexpected venues. EventoLoop lists both private and commercial spaces as well as a curated selection of service providers from caterers to photographers, making it a one-stop-shop for all your event needs.
EventoLoop for Space Owners
How does EventoLoop work for space owners?

Space owners sign up with some basic information about the space, its location, and their contact info. After approval by the EventoLoop team, they add more information about the space, upload pictures, and select the types of events they welcome in their space. When an event organizer contacts a space owner, they will reply with further information about availability and prices. The space owner is responsible for making sure the space is use-ready before every event. EventoLoop will handle payment processing and all other backend details to guarantee a safe and secure exchange all around. EventoLoop will also facilitate reviews following the event to make sure wonderful spaces and hosts get proper notice.

 Tools for EventoLoop Owners
What is a booking request?

A booking request takes place when an organizer/customer has committed to your space and event dates, and has initiated the booking process for your space. All you need to do is accept the request and the event is instantly booked. No need to create a quote or message the organizer/customer , if you don’t require it. Once you hit ‘Accept’, your space and contact details will automatically be shared with the organizer/customer.

How is a booking request different from an inquiry?

With a booking request the organizer/customer has committed to your space and has held the reservation with a credit card. The amount held is that of the default price on your space page. The organizer may or may not need to message with you about the event. An inquiry is sent by an organizer/customer who is interested in your space, but may still have questions and/or require a site visit before committing to it. In this case it is up to you to create a quote in order for the booking process to start. (The organizer/customer has the option to send a booking request at any time during your conversation).

How do I respond to a booking request?

It’s easy! Just click on the booking request from your inbox, and select “Accept”. The event will automatically be confirmed and booked. You have a few other options if you can’t accept the request at this time: Create a custom quote to adjust the price or times of the event Message the organizer if you have any questions before accepting the request Decline the event to let the organizer know you can’t accommodate the event.

How long to I have to respond to a booking request?

You have 2 business days to respond to a booking request (either accept, send a new quote, message the organizer or decline the request). If you fail to send a response, the opportunity will expire and this will impact your response rate & time. As a result your search ranking will be affected.

I’d like to host the event, but my space is unavailable during the time the organizer requested. What should I do?

The booking request is valid for 2 business days and you can message the organizer and see if another date or time works for them. If you’ve agreed on a new time, select “create custom quote” and make the relevant adjustments to the event details. The organizer can then book and pay for the event as usual.

I’d like to host the event, but the price needs to be adjusted. What should I do?

You can select “create custom quote” and make the relevant adjustments to the event details as well as add a note to the quote about why you’re adjusting it. The organizer can then book and pay for the event as usual. You can message the organizer at any time to let them know about this needed change.

I’ve been messaging the organizer, but the booking request has expired. Have I lost the opportunity?

Since you’ve responded to the organizer within 2 business days window, you can still book the opportunity. Since the booking request has expired, you will have to send a new quote for the booking to take place.

I declined a booking request - does that impact my response rate?

Nope! As long as you somehow respond to the organizer, your response rate and time aren’t negatively impacted. You can always follow up with the organizer and tell them why you can’t accommodate their event. You cannot decline three straight requests. It may lead to exclusion from the listings.

What are EventoLoop’s messaging & booking policies?

We understand that at times contact information needs to be shared in order to secure an event. However, to ensure that the benefits of EventoLoop are received by all parties, and for the protection of our users' privacy and security, we do require that the following set of rules be followed when communicating on EventoLoop: All conversation surrounding a booking should take place on the EventoLoop platform, except during a site visit or phone call. Get as many details about the event to ensure you can move forward with it, before scheduling an in-person meeting or phone call. When a site visit or call are deemed necessary, a date and time for the meeting should be agreed upon. Once this agreement is reached, the space address and phone numbers can be shared to facilitate the meeting. Immediately after a site visit or phone call, the conversation should return to EventoLoop with a message, quote or quote request. When it comes time to confirm the rental, all payment* must be processed through the EventoLoop quote system. This ensures, the benefits of a EventoLoop booking for all parties. When these policies are adhered to, the EventoLoop team can support you throughout the booking process and ensure that: Payment is verified and secure You’re covered by our insurance policy We can help resolve and manage disputes should they arise.

 Listing a space on EventoLoop
Is there a cost for listing a space on EventoLoop ?

There is no cost to listing your space on EventoLoop. Splacer only charges a service fee on booked events.

Do you need to be exclusive to EventoLoop to list?

No, we do not require Space Owners to be solely exclusive with our website.

What information is included in my listing page?

Your listing page will provide event organizers the basics: schedule, rates, amenities, rules, etc. But you also have an opportunity to share what makes your space special. Tell organizers how you found your space, what you’ve done with it, and any other details you think will make the space stand out.

How can I edit and add photos to my listing?

You can edit your picture but EventoLoop will verify it before making it live.

What makes a successful listing?

1.Great Photos High resolution photographs of your space at various angles, in bright lighting will catch the attention of event organizers. Landscape orientation looks best on our platform. 2. Prompt response to event inquires. We have found that space owners who respond to event inquires quickly have the highest rate of success on the website. 3. Competitive pricing

I offer in-house catering and beverage services — can I include that in my listing?

Our service revolves specifically around the rental of the space itself, but you are free to include that information in your description and communicate with the event organizer regarding special services you provide. Splacer will remain uninvolved in negotiations between you and the organizer on food and beverage pricing and other special services (like lighting equipment for example) and will not take a service fee off any additional charges.

Can my space be displayed higher up on the search page result?

The search result page is based on the popularity of the space. Spaces that generally are most popular are ones that have a competitive hourly rate, and high resolution images that highlight the beauty and essence of the space.

Can I choose to stop listing my space? Is there a penalty?

There is no obligation to remain on the site once you list your space. You can drop out at any time without penalty. Just send a message to info@EventoLoop.co and tell EventoLoop why you wish to stop listing. Your listing will be deactivated within 2 business days.

 Fees and Payments
Where can I see my payment status?

Payment status can be found in your event page under the Account > Transaction history.

Does EventoLoop charge anything?

EventoLoop charges owners a 6% service fee for each booking generated through EventoLoop.com . That amount is taken out of the rate you specify in a quote for an event.

How should I set my rates?

We give you freedom in choosing the rates you set based on rent per hour / price per plate basis.

How / when do I get paid?

You will get paid with in 24 hrs after the event.

Do I get paid if an event was cancelled after it was already Booked?

Space owners are paid for cancelled events according to their choice of cancellation policy. EventoLoop will issue a refund to the client in the appropriate amount.
