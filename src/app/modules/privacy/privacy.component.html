PRIVACY POLICY

We recognize that your privacy is very important. This privacy policy covers SurprizGift’s policies on the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use, access and initiate transactions on www.eventoloop.com and the SurprizGift mobile applications (collectively the “Platform”) or anytime you interact with SurprizGift. Each time you use the Platform, you consent to the collection, use and storage of the collected information as described in this Privacy Policy. Please read it carefully and contact legal@eventoloop.com if you have any questions.

What Information Does SurprizGift Collect On The Platform?

User Account: Users may create an account by providing their email address/mobile phone and password. Creating an account is optional and users may access the Platform as a guest at any time. 

Purchases: When you make transactions on the Platform, SurprizGift will collect payment information as follows:

Payment: SurpriseGift requires users to input the following payment information: credit or debit card information, first and last name, billing address, card expiration date, and three-digit security code.

Email Addresses & Contact Information: Users may also provide their email address and/or other contact information to SurprizGift to subscribe to its newsletters, to contact us through the Platform with questions about our Platform and Services, or to reset their password. Users can opt-out of newsletter communications through the unsubscribe link in that email.

Bookings: Inorder complete a booking as a guest or add a new space to the platform as a host, user has to submit certain Identification/legal documents such as Aadhar, Passport, proof of ownership of the space or other relevant documents. 

Cookies: SurprizGift utilizes cookie technology to gather information on internet use in order to serve you more effectively. Usage of cookies is in no way linked to personally identifiable information. You can set your browser to remove or reject cookies; however some Platform features or Services may not work properly without cookies.

Is Information Collected By Or Disclosed To Third Parties By Using The Platform?

Credit or Debit Card Information: SurprizGift does not itself store debit or credit card information on its servers. SurprizGift uses Razorpay to process payments on the Platform. You should review Razorpay's Privacy Policy for more information on their data collection and use practices.

Payment Account Set-Up: Hosts will be required to provide their bank account information and social security number in order to receive payment for services booked through the Platform. Payment processing services for users (both guest and host) on the Platform are provided by Razorpay and are subject to the Razorpay Account Agreement, which includes the Razorpay Terms of Service. Information is collected and used in accordance with Razorpay’s Privacy Policy.

Customer Support: Our customer support service is powered by Facebook Messenger. Users should review the Facebook Privacy Policy for more detail about the information collected and utilized by Facebook. 

SMS Log-In & Notifications: The Platform utilizes Solv Technologies’s 2factor.in to enable SMS notifications relating to user activity on the platform, upcoming events and bookings. Data is collected by in accordance with Solv Technologies Privacy Policy.

Anonymous Data - MixPanel Analytics: SurprizGift uses MixPanel to learn how our customers use the Platform and Services. MixPanel utilizes cookies and web beacons to track the manner in which customers use and interact with MixPanel’s features, such as the elements that a user has clicked upon, so that we may improve the Services provided to our customers. For more information on MixPanel’s data collection and use practices, see the MixPanel Privacy Policy. You can opt-out of MixPanel’s automatic retention of data collected by sending an email to our support team at help@eventoloop.com. If you get a new computer, install a new browser, erase or otherwise alter your browser's cookie file (including upgrading certain browsers) you may also clear the MixPanel opt-out cookie.

Data Storage: Data is stored on servers maintained by Amazon Web Services. Users should click on the hyperlink for more information about the data collection and privacy policies.

Social Media Log-in and Plug-Ins: Users may log-in with their Facebook credentials. Data is collected by Facebook in accordance with Facebook's Data Policy. Users may also share their SurprizGift experiences or follow SurprizGift on other third party social media sites. Users should review the applicable privacy policies of those sites for more detail about information collected from these sites.

Other Potential Third Party Disclosures: Information may also be disclosed to third parties (1) as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, or similar legal process, (2) when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request, or (3) if SurprizGift is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of its assets.

We will use commercially reasonable efforts to notify users (i) about law enforcement or court ordered requests for data unless otherwise prohibited by law, or (ii) if your personal information is to be disclosed or transferred and/or becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

How Is Collected Information Used And Stored?

Information collected may be utilized to (i) communicate with our users regarding their booking requests, upcoming bookings and events, (ii) communicate with users regarding their account, including account activation and password reset instructions, (iii) facilitate or provide information regarding our Services; (iv) to communicate material changes to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy; and/or (v) help us maintain and improve the Services offered.

SurprizGift will also utilize your email when you subscribe to our newsletter or wish to reset your password. You can unsubscribe to our newsletter via the opt-out link within the email.

How Does SurprizGift Comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act?

We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13 without parental consent. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with information without their consent, he or she should contact us at legal@eventoloop.com. We will delete such information from our files within a reasonable time.

How Long Does SurprizGift Retain Information Collected?

SurprizGift will retain account and transaction data for a commercially reasonable time and as we deem necessary to provide our services to our users. If you would like us to delete your registered account information, please contact us at legal@eventoloop.com and we will respond in a reasonable time.

What Is SurprizGift’s Security Policy?

We have implemented reasonable administrative, technical and physical security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, destruction or alteration. For example, we limit access to this information to authorized employees and contractors who need to know that information in order to operate, develop or improve our Platform. However, although we endeavor to provide reasonable security for information we process and maintain, no security system can ever by 100% secure.

Please see the AWS Cloud Security Policy for more information on its security practices.

SurprizGift utilizes only PCI-DSS compliant third party payment processors to ensure the security of your personal information. Users should review Razorpay’s Security Policy for more information on their security practices.

How Does SurprizGift Respond To “Do Not Track” Signals?

“Do Not Track” is a feature enabled on some browsers that sends a signal to request that a web application disable its tracking or cross-site user tracking. At present, SurpriseGift does not respond to or alter its practices when a Do Not Track signal is received.

How Will I Be Notified Of Changes To Your Privacy Policy?

If SurprizGift makes material changes to its Privacy Policy, it will notify you by: (i) changing the Effective Date at the top of the Privacy Policy, (ii) sending an email to its account holders, and/or (iii) adding a statement to the Platform.

You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.
